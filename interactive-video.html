How it works

Step 1

Paste & load

Drop any YouTube URL. The safe embed loads — no related-video clutter, no ads in the sidebar, no "up next" to derail students.

Step 2

Add questions at any second

Pause at the moment you want to check understanding. Click Add question here. Choose Multiple choice, Open-ended, or a Poll — timestamp is captured automatically.

Step 3

Share & track

Copy the share link for students, or export to SCORM/Common Cartridge for your LMS. Students can't skip past a question — the video pauses until they answer.
