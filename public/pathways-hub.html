How it works

From first spark to confident mastery

A simple loop that connects what learners need, what teachers plan, and what parents can support at home.

Discover

Pick a goal in Pathways Learn. Get a roadmap that matches the learner's level and curriculum.

Plan

Teachers and parents open E² EduHub to generate lesson plans, weekly grids, and rubrics in seconds.

Align

Map every step to standards in the Frameworks Navigator — IB, Common Core, Egyptian 2.0, Al-Azhar, and more.

Reflect

Use E² rubrics and exit tickets to adjust. The teacher adapts first — never the student.

About Pathways

Every student has the right to understand.

Pathways was built by Dina El Ahl as a holistic home for the E² philosophy — a teaching framework that puts adaptation on the teacher, not blame on the student. The suite is free, open, and made to support educators and families everywhere.
