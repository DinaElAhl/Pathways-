Step 2 of 4

Choose Your Tracks

اختر مساراتك

RQAP certifies two independent constructs. You may take one track, the other, or both. Both tracks are recommended for the full Level 2 credential.

Track 1 — Language

مسار اللغة القرآنية
  • Vocabulary & trilateral roots
  • Classical grammar & morphology
  • Verse comprehension
  • Constructed Arabic response

Track 2 — Tajweed

مسار التجويد والتلاوة
  • Theory: makharij & sifaat
  • Rules of noon, meem, madd
  • Applied identification
  • Practical recitation (audio)

Note for the demo: This sample administration includes a reduced item set from each section so you can experience the full instrument in approximately 30 minutes.
