The E² Teaching Framework

Every Student Has the Right to Understand. A four-pillar framework for effective, engaging, equitable teaching — and the spine behind every flipped video and suggested path on Pathways.

Read the framework →
Pillar 1

🧱 Lesson Design

The 5-part blueprint: Hook · Teach · Practice · Connect · Reflect. The backbone of every strong lesson.

 Pillar 2

🎯 Differentiation

Auditory, visual, kinesthetic, tiered tasks, cultural responsiveness, and real student choice.

 Pillar 3

✨ Engagement

Six levers: curiosity, active participation, purpose, feedback, gamification, routine/novelty balance.

 Pillar 4

🤝 Classroom Culture

Relationships first, co-created norms, restorative practices, a welcoming environment for every learner.
