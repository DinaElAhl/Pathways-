Why Flipped Studio looks like this

Flipping isn't just "watch this at home." Real flipped learning structures the experience so students come to class primed, curious, and ready to do the hard cognitive work with the teacher. Flipped Studio follows the E² 5-part blueprint:

Hook (2 min)

A curiosity-sparking question or scenario so watching feels like it matters.

Teach (10-15 min)

Chunked content delivery with chapter markers so students can rewatch the exact part they missed.

Practice (15-20 min)

Pause-point checks and guided questions — active, not passive watching.

Connect (5 min)

Real-world tie-ins that answer "why does this matter to me?"

Reflect (5 min)

An exit ticket that lets students show they understood — and flags what they didn't.
